### Suggestions
- The chart color variables (`chart-1` through `chart-5`) are not defined in the `.dark` block. If charts are used in dark mode, they'll fall back to light-mode colors. Consider adding dark chart colors in a follow-up.
- Could add a "System" option to the settings page selector for users who prefer OS-level theme switching (currently disabled via `enableSystem={false}`, which is fine per spec).
